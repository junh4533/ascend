Marketing

The Marketing Committee devises marketing strategies to increase Ascend's presence on campus. Members will design flyers, manage Ascend's social media platforms, design exclusive merchandise, and learn how to use marketing tools such as Adobe Creative Suite and Canva.

Technology

The Technology Committee maintains Ascend's website and launches features to better convey the range of opportunities available at Ascend. Members will build foundational knowledge in coding and programming and learn how to use industry standard tools such as Github and Slack.

Fundraising

The Fundraising Committee hosts the fundraisers that make other Ascend Events possible. Members will brainstorm creative ideas for fundraisers, create fundraising materials, and connect with students to inform them about what it means to be a part of the Ascend community.

Professional

The Professional Committee plans professional development events including resume/cover letter workshops, networking sessions, and professional panels. Members will have the chance to attend exclusive office visits and learn about different professional opportunities.

Legacy Leadership Program


The Legacy Leadership Program (LLP) is an intensive 10-week career development and mentorship program, where mentees go through a series of high-level workshops and training to become young professionals. LLP provides the necessary tools to enhance leadership and gain insight into the business world including resume-writing, networking, public speaking, case study management, stock pitch and start-up presentation. Through one-to-one mentorship, mentees have the opportunity to build a bond with their mentors and improve professional communication skills.



Weekly Web Workshop (W3)


Whether it is machine learning used for financial algorithms or VBA applied to accounting formulas, technology has become an integral part of the business world. In this 8-week series of web development workshops, you'll grasp the fundamentals of coding and programming through building a full-fledged personal website. This website can be used as a portfolio to showcase your technical expertise, an e-commerce platform to sell goods/services, or simply a personal website to share your story with the world. Throughout the course, you'll also learn how to use industry-leading tools like Visual Studio, Github, Trello, and Slack. But more importantly, you'll acquire essential coding and programming skills that will give you a competitive edge in any industry.
